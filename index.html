Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Bundlify — FBT & Bundles ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when merchants install and use the App on their Shopify store, and how the App handles data related to the merchant's customers.

1. Information We Collect

When you install the App, we access certain data from your Shopify store in order to provide the App's functionality:

  • Store information: shop domain, store owner contact details, and plan information.
  • Product data: product titles, variants, prices, and inventory levels, used to build bundles and frequently-bought-together suggestions.
  • Order data: order line items and totals, used to calculate co-purchase patterns and attribute revenue to bundles and discounts.
  • Theme data: limited access required to install and display the App's storefront widgets (theme app extensions).

We do not collect payment card details, and we do not access customer personal information beyond what is strictly necessary for order attribution (e.g., order ID and line items). We do not sell or rent any data to third parties.

2. How We Use Information

  • To display product bundles, cross-sell widgets, and discounts on your storefront.
  • To calculate and display analytics such as attach rate and revenue generated by the App's features.
  • To bill for paid subscription plans via the Shopify Billing API.
  • To provide customer support when you contact us.
  • To send you service-related communications, such as feature updates or account notices.

3. Data Storage and Security

Store and order data is stored on secure, access-controlled infrastructure. Access tokens are encrypted at rest. We retain data only for as long as the App remains installed on your store, plus a limited period afterward as required for legal or accounting purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell merchant or customer data. We may share limited data with third-party service providers strictly to operate the App (for example, hosting providers or email delivery services), each bound by confidentiality obligations. We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Merchant Rights and Data Deletion

When you uninstall the App, we delete your shop's stored data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. You may request earlier deletion or a copy of your data by contacting us using the details below.

6. Customer Data Requests (GDPR/CCPA)

In compliance with Shopify's mandatory compliance webhooks, we respond to the following requests:

  • Customer data request: a customer's request to receive the personal data we hold about them.
  • Customer data erasure: a customer's request to delete their personal data.
  • Shop data erasure: deletion of a shop's data 48 hours after uninstalling the App (or as required).

7. Cookies and Tracking

The App's storefront widgets may use minimal, non-invasive tracking (such as anonymous session identifiers) solely to measure feature performance (e.g., bundle attach rate). This does not include cross-site advertising tracking.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by business merchants and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 16.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
